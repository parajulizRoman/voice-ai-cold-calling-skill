## Instant Onboarding: Snapshots

Build once, onboard in under an hour.

1. Create a master sub-account with all automations, pipelines, email templates, workflows, and forms pre-configured
2. Save it as a Snapshot
3. For each new client: load Snapshot → customize branding → connect their accounts → live

**What used to take days now takes under an hour.** Every new client saves time vs the last.

For Qritim, create one Snapshot per service:
- Social Media Management Snapshot (content calendar, comment-to-DM, lead magnet)
- Website + Chat Snapshot (AI site template, chat widget, booking automation)
- Reputation Management Snapshot (review requests, smart routing, auto-replies)

## The $97 Downsell (Stop Losing Revenue)

When a client wants to cancel, they usually still use SOMETHING you built. Don't let them walk away for free.

**The downsell script:**
> "Totally understand if you want to pause the full service. But the website, the AI chat widget, and all the automations we built — those are still live and working for you. We can drop you to a $97/month hosting and maintenance plan. Everything stays up, the AI keeps answering, the reviews keep coming. You just don't get new content each month. Sound fair?"

Most say yes. Even cancelled clients keep paying.

**Why this works:**
- It's $97 vs $0 — not a hard sell
- They already see the value (the site, the widget, the automations)
- Turning it completely off feels like losing something
- It costs you nothing to keep it running

## Lead Connector App (Silent Retention)

Get every client to install the Lead Connector mobile app.

Every notification — new lead, new message, new booking, new review — is a reminder that you're providing value. They see momentum in real time, on their phone, every day.

**Onboarding script:**
> "One last thing — download this app called Lead Connector. It shows you every lead, message, and booking coming through in real time. Takes 30 seconds to set up."

This single action cuts churn significantly because the value is now visible daily, not just in a monthly report.

## Consolidated Reports (Look Like a $100K Agency)

Don't screenshot 5 tools and paste into Google Docs. That looks amateur.

Use GHL's dashboard to show everything in one place: calls, leads, bookings, reviews, social media stats, email opens, pipeline movement.

**Monthly client report script:**
> "Here's your dashboard — every lead that came in, every call that was answered, every booking, every review, every post that went out. All in one place."

This makes you look more professional than agencies 10x your size. Trust goes up. Cancellations go down.

## The Social Media Selling System (Complete Loop)

From the video — 4 steps that form a complete lead generation machine:

1. **Content**: Ask AI creates + schedules single-image and carousel posts (3 prompts, no Canva)
2. **Lead Magnet**: AI Studio builds interactive mini-apps (not just PDFs) — quizzes, calculators, assessments
3. **Comment-to-DM**: Auto-DM anyone who comments a trigger word with the lead magnet link
4. **Capture**: Name, email, phone captured → CRM → pipeline → follow-up

**Example for a florist:** Post "5 flowers that last longest in summer" → carousel ends with "Comment BLOOM for our free flower care guide" → DM sends AI Studio quiz → captures lead info → pipelines them.

This is what Qritim sells as the social media package. Not just posting — a full selling system.
