## A2P Registration Process (Quick Reference)

### One-Time Setup
1. Settings → Phone Integration → Sub-Account Settings
2. Link account to Lead Connector
3. Fill Business Profile: website, type, industry, EIN, authorized rep

### AI Registration Prompt
```
"I want to send text messages from this sub-account. I need to register and 
get approved for A2P, the brand and the campaign. Can you please help me 
register as fast as possible by providing all the sample messaging and 
information needed? Only ask me the absolutely essential questions and make 
your best assumptions about everything else."
```

### Timeline
- ~5 business days for approval (carriers, not GHL)
- Set up EARLY — don't wait until campaign day

### If Rejected
- 90% of rejections: legal business name doesn't match IRS EIN exactly
- Fix: find exact name on CP575 letter, tell AI to update, resubmit
- Check status anytime: ask AI "Check my A2P status"

### While Waiting
- Build all automations now, flip them on when approved
- Use email for follow-ups in the meantime
