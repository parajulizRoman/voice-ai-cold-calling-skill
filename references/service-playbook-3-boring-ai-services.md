## The Core Philosophy

## Service 1: AI-Powered Social Media Management — $500-1,000/mo

### The Problem
7 out of 10 local businesses haven't posted in months. Their Instagram is dead. Their Facebook looks abandoned. They're too busy running the business to make content.

### The Qritim Solution
Using GHL's built-in AI:
1. **Content Planning**: Ask AI plans a full month of posts — ideas, captions, best days/times
2. **Design**: AI designs carousels using the business's brand colors and fonts
3. **Scheduling**: Auto-schedules to Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Google Business
4. **Comment-to-DM Automation**: When someone comments, GHL slides into their DMs automatically
5. **Lead Magnets**: AI Studio builds quizzes/assessments that capture name, email, phone
6. **Auto-Reports**: Social Planner statistics show the client what's working
7. **Ad Boosting**: Boost top-performing posts with a small budget

### The Pitch
"You don't create a single post. You just wake up to a consistent, professional-looking feed that brings in customers."

### Pricing
- $500/mo — standard
- $1,000/mo — with custom posts or AI videos

## Service 2: AI-Powered Website Design — $500/mo

### The Problem
Small businesses either have no website or one from 2010. They hate dealing with web designers — slow and expensive.

### The Qritim Solution
1. **AI Website Builder**: One prompt builds a full site (homepage, services, about, booking)
2. **Chat Widget**: Voice + text AI answers visitors 24/7
3. **Booking Automation**: Confirms bookings, sends reminders 24hr + 2hr before, thanks them after, rebooks no-shows automatically

### The Pitch (CRITICAL)
**Don't say:** "I'll build you a website."
**Say:** "I'll build you a website that books appointments while you sleep."

That sells so much better because now it's not a cost — it's a machine that brings them money.

### Pricing
- $500/mo flat (monthly because the chat widget and booking system keep working)

## Service 3: AI-Powered Reputation Management — $300/mo

### The Problem
Reviews are everything for local business. More 5-star reviews = higher Google ranking = more customers. Most businesses never ask for reviews and never respond to the ones they have.

### The Qritim Solution
1. **Auto Review Requests**: After every appointment, texts the customer asking how it went
2. **Smart Routing**: Happy (4-5 stars) → sent to Google to leave public review. Unhappy (1-3 stars) → sent to private form, routed to your team
3. **Reviews AI**: Auto-responds to every review so the business looks active
4. **GBP Optimization**: Cleans up Google Business Profile for local ranking
5. **Upsell path**: SEO (more custom, more hands-on, bigger money)

### The Pitch
"No business is ever going to turn off a system that keeps feeding them five-star reviews."

### Pricing
- $300/mo
